About Us

Our Mission

The Calgary Palestinian Council (CPC) is dedicated to being a leading social, political, and lobbying organization in Calagry, committed to promoting the rights, heritage, and well-being of the Palestinian people. Through outreach, education, and advocacy, we strive to build bridges of understanding and solidarity while working towards a just and peaceful resolution of the Palestinian-Israeli conflict.

Our Vission

Empowering the Palestinian community in Calgary and fostering understanding, respect, and support for the Palestinian cause throughout Canada.
